Why Chuck Adams is one of the best bowhunters

As the first person known to complete the Super Slam, the pinacle of all hunting achievements;taking all 29 big game animals in North America, he is one of the bowhunting legends along with Fred Bear, Randy Ulmer, Ben Pearson, Doug Easton, and many many more. He is very succesful and very well known for doing this, they even had a bow named the Super Slam after he completed this challenge.
